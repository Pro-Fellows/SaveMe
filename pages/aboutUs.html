Our Mission

we are a bunch of concerned citizens that we want to save every single life we can, we noticed from our personal experience that some people die due to lack of simple blood donation A donation that harms no one, can save up to three lives. our mission is reaching out every home, try to convince those who are able to contribute to reach out to all those who are in need we want to be the hub that can reach out to each individual who is willing to help, or those who urgently or regularly need these donations. we are the advocates for this important cause we want to help! and to be involved in saving lives do you want to join?

Our Vision

making blood donation as a integrated culture in our society
we want to decrease the deaths due to lack of blood donations
